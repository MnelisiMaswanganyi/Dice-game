GAME RULES :
• Press 'Start Game' button to start a new round.
• The player on 'Red' roll the dice first by pressing
the 'Roll dice' button then 'Hold button' to give the next player a chance.
• If the number rolled is 1 , no score is recorded
and the next player takes the turn.
• If the number rolled is any other than 1 ,
the score is recorded and the next player takes the turn.
• The first player to get to the score sum of 12 , WINS.
